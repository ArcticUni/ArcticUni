Nature's Most Complete
Marine Mineral

In the pristine waters of Northern Norway, beneath the dancing aurora borealis, sea urchins thrive in one of the world's purest marine environments. Over millennia, these remarkable creatures have concentrated the ocean's mineral wealth into their shells — a natural calcium carbonate matrix enriched with essential trace minerals.

ArcticUNI captures this natural complexity. Unlike synthetic calcium supplements, our marine mineral complex preserves the full spectrum of naturally occurring minerals — calcium, magnesium, potassium, phosphorus, iron, zinc, and many more — exactly as nature intended.

Why ArcticUNI

The Arctic Advantage

01

Bone Health & Strength

Calcium is essential for maintaining strong bones and teeth. ArcticUNI delivers 250 mg of marine-derived calcium per capsule — a highly bioavailable form your body can readily absorb and utilize.

02

Natural Marine Source

Unlike synthetic calcium supplements, ArcticUNI is derived entirely from Arctic sea urchins. This whole-food marine source preserves the natural mineral matrix, providing calcium in the form nature designed.

03

Rich Trace Mineral Profile

Beyond calcium, each capsule contains magnesium, potassium, phosphorus, iron, zinc, and a spectrum of trace minerals — a complete marine mineral complex working in natural synergy.

04

Cardiovascular Support

The synergistic combination of calcium, magnesium, and potassium supports healthy cardiovascular function. These essential minerals work together to help maintain normal blood pressure and heart rhythm.

05

Cellular Protection

Sea urchins contain naturally occurring antioxidant compounds, including unique pigments (spinochromes) that help protect cells from oxidative stress and support the body's natural defense systems.

06

Pure & Sustainable

Sustainably harvested from the pristine Arctic waters at 69° North. No additives, no preservatives, no GMOs. Just pure marine minerals in a vegetable capsule — nothing more.

Origin

From Arctic Waters
to You

ArcticUNI originates from the cold, clean waters off the coast of Northern Norway, far above the Arctic Circle. Here, minimal pollution and extreme conditions produce sea urchins with an exceptionally pure and consistent mineral profile.

Every batch is fully traceable — from the specific harvesting location in the Arctic Sea to the finished capsule in your hands. We maintain complete transparency across our entire supply chain, ensuring the purity and quality that defines ArcticUNI.
